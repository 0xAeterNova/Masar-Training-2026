# Governance, Risk and Compliance

This directory contains the **Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC)** sessions for **Masar Training 2026**.

## Overview

The GRC track focuses on the strategic and organizational side of cybersecurity. It introduces the core concepts of governance, compliance, risk management, and resilience, helping build an understanding of how security is managed at both the operational and business levels.

## Included Sessions

- **Session 22:** Governance, Compliance & Frameworks
- **Session 23:** Risk Management in Practice
- **Session 24:** Business Continuity & Resilience

## Purpose

This section is designed to provide a strong foundation in governance, risk, and compliance by covering the principles, frameworks, and practices that support effective security management and organizational resilience.

## Note

These sessions help connect cybersecurity with policy, decision-making, and long-term resilience, making them an essential part of a complete security learning path.
