# Session 4: Exercise

## Question

The management team at Bazaarjo has decided to conduct a security assessment of their network and cloud infrastructure. However, they are unsure whether to choose a penetration test or a red team engagement. To help them decide, a specialized security vendor provided two sample reports: one from a penetration test and another from a red team exercise.

1. Your **task** is to review both reports and write down your observations on the key differences between them.

2. After reviewing the reports, create a short briefing (maximum one paragraph) that you would present to Bazaarjo’s executive management.

3. The briefing should clearly explain, in non-technical language, what each type of assessment offers and why these differences matter for business risk.

Deliverable: PDF report.
