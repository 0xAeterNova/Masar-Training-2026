# Batches

This directory contains the **session exercises and practical tasks** for **Masar Training 2026**.

## Overview

The batches section represents the hands-on part of the training. It is where the practical exercises are organized to support the concepts covered during the sessions and help apply them in a more realistic way.

## Purpose

These exercises are designed to strengthen understanding through practice, problem-solving, and direct interaction with technical tasks related to the course topics.

## What This Section Includes

- Session-based exercises
- Practical labs and activities
- Applied tasks connected to the training material
- Step-by-step work that supports hands-on learning

## Note

This section is intended to be the practical side of the program, where theoretical knowledge is reinforced through real exercises and guided implementation.
